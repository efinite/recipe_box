====== Vanilla Glaze ======
Ref(s): https://www.bakefromscratch.com/birthday-cake-scones-vanilla-glaze/

===== Yield =====
?

===== Ingredients =====
  * 0.5 cup (60g) confectioners' sugar
  * 1 Tbsp (15g) whole milk
  * 0.5 tsp (2g) vanilla extract
  * 1/4 tsp kosher salt

===== Preparation =====
  - In a small bowl, whisk together all ingredients until smooth. Glaze should form a sturdy ribbon when drizzled from a spoon.
  - Refrigerate.

===== Serving =====
  * Over a nice scone, hot out of the oven.
