====== No-Knead Bread ======
Ref(s): https://www.thekitchn.com/how-to-make-noknead-bread-home-109343

===== Yield =====
1 Loaf

===== Ingredients =====
  * 1 lb (~3 cups) all-purpose flour
  * 1 tsp salt
  * 3/4 tsp active dry yeast (no sugar/activation)
  * 1 1/2 cups lukewarm water

===== Equipment =====
  * Dutch oven
  * Parchment paper
  * Kitchen towel

===== Preparation =====
  - In a large bowl, combine flour, salt, yeast. Add water, stirring until homogeneous.
  - Cover bowl with towel and set in warm place to rise until doubled (6-8 hrs for normal active yeast, 2 hrs for rapid rise yeast)
  - Turn dough onto a lightly floured piece of parchment. Fold dough over on itself at least once. Shape into a round ball. Cover with a kitchen towel and let rise again (1 hr).

===== Baking =====
  - Preheat oven to 450Â°F, allowing dutch oven to heat up inside.
  - Transfer dough on parchment into dutch oven, cover with lid, and bake (20-25 min; times will vary).
  - Remove dutch oven lid and continue baking (4-5 min; times will vary).
  - Immediately remove loaf from dutch oven and allow to cool.

===== Note(s) =====
  * Freezes well. Slice loaf before freezing.
